Zaatari Refugee Camp

Zaatari is home to nearly 1.5 million refugees of the civil war in Syria. It is a relief effort of the United Nations under the High Commisioner for Refugees. The United Nations is working to make this camp a welcoming home for these victims of war while also finding them new homes away from the strife of their own nation.

Zaatari is comprised of 12 districts and organized as a large city. It is outfitted with not only homes, but hospitals, schools, markets, even barber shops and tailors. There are schools available in the American system as well as the Saudi tradition. Religious centers are scattered all over the map and several are available in each district.
